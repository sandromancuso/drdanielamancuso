Travelling to us

By Train

London Waterloo Station to Windsor & Eton Riverside Station - approximately 50 minutes on the train.

Walk out of station following signs for Windsor Castle turning left up the hill, castle on your left, at the top turn right down Peascod St, walk all the way to the bottom until you reach a crossing (pub on the right called Crosses Corner) cross over the road turning right down Clarence Rd (past mini roundabout) Dorset Rd is the 1st left off Clarence Rd.

London Paddington to Slough Station Approximately 22 minutes on the fast train. We recommend you take a Taxi from Slough Station to Windsor, (10 mins in a taxi)

National rail enquiries service: 08457 484950

By Air

Heathrow Airport is just 15 minutes by car (please contact us and we will be delighted to arrange a car to meet you).

By Car

We are situated 25 miles west of London.

M4 - exit at junction 6, following signs for Windsor, taking you onto a dual carriageway, stay on this until the very end, at the roundabout take your 1st exit along Clarence Rd, go through 2 sets of traffic lights, after the 2nd set of lights turn right onto Dorset Rd.

M3 - exit junction 3 (Bagshot)/ A322 / A332

M25 - exit junction 13 (Egham)/ A30
