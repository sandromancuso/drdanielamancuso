NEW TECHNOLOGIES

Dr Mancuso utilizes many of the newest technologies available in dentistry for your treatment. She uses special operating microscopes, adapted for dentistry, during all phases of your root canal treatment. These precise instruments allow her to visualize fine details, up to 20x the magnification of the naked eye, inside of your tooth.

She also uses digital radiography to take films of your teeth, which reduces radiation by as much as 80 percent and digital cat scanning machine to better diagnose and treat your tooth. This machine gives us the ability to see your tooth in three dimensions as well as take cross sections of your tooth to look for dental infections.

Dental research shows 34% greater ability to identify infections using this machine as compared to standard dental x-rays.
