About

Dr Daniela Nardi Mancuso BDS (Hon), MSc, PhD, Specialist in Prosthodontics

Dr Mancuso is both UK and Brazilian registered Specialist in Prosthodontics. Her expertise includes advanced endodontics, advanced restorative dentistry and full mouth reconstruction.

Lectures and hands-on courses have been presented nationally and worldwide and she has published her work in renowned journals.

Currently, she is Clinical Lecturer in both Endodontics and Prosthodontics at Queen Mary University of London, Associate Clinical Teacher in Endodontics at The University of Warwick and also Invited Professor at UNESP - Sao Paulo State University in Brazil.

She is also responsible for Endodontics and part of the restorative team at the Windsor Centre for Advanced Dentistry, Windsor, UK.

Received her Bachelor of Dental Surgery degree with honours from the Sao Paulo State University (UNESP), considered to be one of the top dental universities in Brazil. She was also the first dentist to receive the prestigious Maillefer Honours and Distinction award for her outstanding work in endodontics. Following her graduation she enrolled into an extensive postgraduate program in which she received her Masters Degree and PhD in Prosthodontics from UNESP and completed her specialization in Prosthodontics from the same university. Dr Mancuso also expanded her expertise in periodontics to complete her knowledge and skills in Restorative Dentistry and because of her outstanding clinical understanding and ability she was invited to work as a Clinical Lecturer in periodontal surgery at UNESP. She also helped in the foundation of the Maxillofacial Prosthodontic Department in the Oral Oncological Centre of UNESP, where she lectured for many years.
